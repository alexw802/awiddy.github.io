Who We Are

Experts with residential and reverse mortages

Stephan Wiederkehr has been in the Mortgage Lending Industry for over a decade and a licensed Mortgage Broker since 2005. Born and raised in Switzerland, he graduated Summa Cum Laude from the University of California at Berkeley.

Stephan understands that every loan transaction is as unique as is the financial and credit profile of each individual client. Matching a client’s specific situation and goals with the right loan program is his mission and excellence in execution is his daily goal.

What Stephan enjoys most is to provide his clients with sound advice and educate them about their mortgage loan options. Stephan loves working as much with first time home buyers as he is working with seasoned real estate investors. Stephan strives to always stay informed about changes in the mortgage industry, to stay ahead of the curve, and to always expand the programs available to his clients. That is why he is one of the few mortgage brokers who specialize in both residential and commercial real estate lending. He understands the intricacies of providing financing for an office or retail property as much as he is seasoned in executing a VA, FHA, or Reverse mortgage transaction.

That is why 99% of his business comes from referrals and repeat clients.

Andrew Martz is a South Bay native graduating from South Torrance High School and California State University Fullerton. Andrew brings over 15 years' experience in the mortgage industry to work for his clients.

Part of Andrew’s success in the industry has undoubtedly been due to his commitment to treating every client individually and striving to find the best product for their own individual needs. His dedication to gathering all the information, being available for his clients, and ensuring a job well done have earned him a solid reputation in the industry and the pleasure of helping many clients over and over again. Andrew is now in the happy position where a great deal of his business comes through repeat business and referrals - a testament to his clients' satisfaction. Andrew’s diversity is apparent in the many types of residential loans he closes for his clients as well as stated income loans, construction loans, VA/FHA Loans, reverse mortgages as well as many types of commercial financing.
